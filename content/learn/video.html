Videos

YouTube channel

Watch all Drools and KIE Community videos on the KIE Community YouTube channel.

The KIE Community Youtube channel is an open space for all topics related business automation. You can find vast set of content about business rules, decisions, processes, resource planning, tooling, engine, trusty AI, and much more. Don't forget to subscribe: you'll be the first to know about the latest and greatest features being created for these projects.

KIE Live

KIE Live is a series of public live streamings for knowledge sharing about any Business Automation topic: business rules, decisions, processes, resource planning, tooling, engine, trusty AI, etc. It is a community effort, therefore, it mentions not only the enterprise products and features but also, the projects that compose Red Hat Process Automation Manager and Decision Manager. It highlights features that might be on the roadmap but are not yet supported in the products.

KIE Drop

A KIE Drop is a short, focused, five minute (or less) video used to promote an idea, one of our KIE projects, or some feature in a project. Do you have an idea for a KIE Drop? Get in contact with us!

KIE Week 2021

KIE Week series -- Exploring Process & Business Automation -- where we’ll take you on a general technical and cultural journey through approaches to integrating process and business automation rules. You’ll delve into workflow through the eyes of some of our core international Red Hat® engineers’ community KIE/Drools projects. And, we’ll round out the week with a roundtable discussion about real-world customer implementations of the Red Hat products built from KIE projects.
Our KIE Week will offer a single one-hour session each day. Join that one that especially resonates with you or register for them all!
